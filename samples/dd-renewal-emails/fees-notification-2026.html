Important Information for Direct Debit Members

If you currently pay by Direct Debit, your subscription will automatically renew on 1st April 2026 (or 1st October for Winter members). You do not need to take any action if you wish to continue your membership.

A separate email with your personal payment schedule will be sent to you in advance, detailing the amount and dates of each monthly collection.

If you wish to cancel your membership, you must notify the club in writing before 1st March 2026 to prevent your Direct Debit from being collected. After this date, you will be liable for the full annual subscription. You can also cancel your Direct Debit at any time by contacting your bank directly.

Payment Methods

Membership subscriptions can be paid by any of the following methods:

• Direct Debit — Spread the cost over 12 monthly payments (available for subscriptions of £125 or more)
• Bank Transfer (BACS) — Pay in full by bank transfer
• Over the Till — Pay at the clubhouse

If you would like to switch to paying by Direct Debit, or have any questions about your subscription, please contact us at subscriptions@AlnmouthVillage.Golf.
